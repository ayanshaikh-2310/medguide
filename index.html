The Problem We're Solving

In India, a huge number of patients don't understand prescriptions, side effects, or dosage — especially in Tier 2/3 cities or rural areas.

Language barriers prevent proper medication adherence

Elderly and non-English speakers face the greatest challenges

Confusion about medication leads to incorrect usage and potential harm
